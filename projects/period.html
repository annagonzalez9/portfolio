Period Web App

Full Stack Development · 2024

Web app designed for menstruating people as the bootcamp final project. Whether you want to keep track of symptoms or discover tips for each phase of your cycle, Period helps you sync with your body.

Use Cases

  • Create/Update/Delete period start & end
  • Create/Update a log of daily symptoms
  • Create/Read reminders
  • Read data about current cycle
  • Read reports of last cycles
  • Read tips regarding the cycle week
